Project Idea

My project idea would be an ecommerce platform/app that allows users to be able to rent items/goods and to lease items themselves as well. I would like to think that it is similar to Ebay/Facebook Marketplace, but with a focus on purely renting and leasing.

It would be an alternative option to rental companies, as well as a way for others to earn extra income/save their money rather than having to go through the trouble of keeping an item and leaving it unused. Increasing the accessibility of things that are expensive to purchase and would only be used occasionally is the main goal of this platform, such as a lawnmowers, deep fryers, surfboards, cameras, etc.

Supposedly, the platform would be based online as a website, and can be accessed through an app as well.

Home Page
At the home page, the user interface is designed to be accessible and easy to use with helpful features such as a search bar, live chat, tiles of equipment catergories, personalized items, and a survey option to go through a series of questionaires to narrow down what is needed for the user.

Search Engine
The search bar will consist of filters to help the user to find what they are looking for. These filters help by sorting available items into catergories such as; item type, location of item, feedback reviews, pricing, and keywords.

Survey Option
The survey will ask the user a series of questions to help them find what they're looking for. These questions will change based on the answer given by the user, and is a step-by-step process to find a specific item. By the end of the survey, items are shown and sorted based on the answers given by the user.

Item Listings
Elements such as a description by the lister, images with zoom-in functionality, pricing, map location, and reviews by other users who have rented from the lister will be shown in the item listing.

Checkout Process
The website will make use of a payment platform such as Cybersource to handle transactions. Offering more payment options is an important aspect of the platform, as it makes it accessible to everyone.

Security Deposits
As a requirement in the process of renting a product, users will have to make a deposit to the lister as a guarantee that the terms and conditions of the listing is abided by.

Intergrated Blog Section
Releasing blogs and articles with helpful tips on how to make use of the platform's features will enable users to utilize it to their needs. These can be relevant to the website or can also be about things not directly related to the platform. Users can have blogs of their own, primarily to announce a product available for rent.

Personalization And Accessibility Features
Users are able to adjust their preffered language, customize their profile, shop and item listings, and blog. In addition to personalization, the platform is designed with accessibility for everyone in mind. To achieve this, multiple colourblind modes and a high contrast setting that can adjust text and menus independently will be provided for users, as well as a text-to-speech narrator option that reads text and other elements aloud. The menus can also be customized, with font sizes for text and UI sizes for menus.

Loyalty Program
For each item a user rents and successfully returns, as well as each item a lister recieves good feedback for, they will recieve an amount of rewards points. They can redeem points for discount codes, onetime use double revenue, and rewards. Users can also earn points by referring the platform to others, if a successful renting of item is made by said others.

Message System
An intergrated messaging system will be available for users to communicate to each other, as well as customer service.

Message System
Based on the feedback a user/lister recieves, they will obtain varying statuses associated with their profile to show that they are trusted.

Summary

As the project needs to take into consideration the budget and key features the platform would have, the best option for the website/app could possibly be software such as BigCommerce or Shopify, seeing that they are also to be mobile compatible when it comes to app development.

Skills that may be required to complete this project could consist of programming and website development knowledge, but thankfully both BigCommerce and Shopify seem user-friendly enough to develop an ecommerce website on. However, if one wanted to have physical servers then BigCommerce would be a suitable option as Shopify websites can only be hosted on the cloud.

The platform aims to provide the general population with an alternative option (rather than rental companies that may be too expensive) To rent everyday items for an affordable price, as well as a way for others to earn extra income from their unused belongings. Things that would normally be expensive and rarely used can now be made more accessible through renting. The platform also makes it a priority to incoporate accessibility features, as everyone should be able to make use of the website/app for their daily necessities.
